About the app

SproutZen (花小绿) helps you track plants in your garden, set care reminders, and optionally use AI for identification, health diagnosis, and stylized artwork.

Current version: 1.0.0

Features

  • My Garden — Add plants, log care, photos, and schedules (stored on device)
  • Care reminders — Local notifications for watering, feeding, pruning, etc.
  • AI Identify — Photo-based plant recognition with care profile
  • AI Diagnose — Photo-based health check with care suggestions; optional location for local climate
  • AI Studio — Turn a plant photo into stylized artwork
  • Plus subscription — Monthly AI quotas; optional top-up packs via in-app purchase

Frequently asked questions

AI identification or diagnosis fails

  • Use a clear photo with the plant centered and well lit.
  • Check your internet connection.
  • AI features require server availability; try again later if you see a rate-limit or server error.
  • Free monthly AI credits may be used up — check Settings → subscription / paywall in the app.

Care reminders not showing

  • Allow notifications for SproutZen in iOS Settings.
  • Enable reminders per care type on the plant’s Care tab.

Location for diagnosis

Location is optional and only used when you start AI diagnosis and grant permission. It improves suggestions with local season and recent weather. You can deny permission and still diagnose.

Subscriptions & restores

  • Purchases are billed by Apple.
  • Use Restore purchases in the app paywall if you reinstalled or changed devices.
  • For refund requests, contact Apple Support — we cannot issue refunds directly.

Delete my data

Garden data is stored locally. Delete plants in the app or uninstall SproutZen to remove on-device data. We do not host a user account database for garden content.

关于本应用

花小绿（SproutZen）提供花园管理、养护提醒， 并可选使用 AI 进行识种、健康诊断与画坊风格化生图。

当前版本： 1.0.0

主要功能

  • 我的花园 — 添加植物、记录养护、相册与计划（数据保存在本机）
  • 养护提醒 — 浇水、施肥等到期本地通知
  • AI 识种 — 拍照识别并生成养护档案
  • AI 诊断 — 拍照健康诊断；可选位置以结合当地气候
  • AI 画坊 — 将植物照片生成风格化形象
  • 订阅与加量包 — 每月 AI 额度，可在应用内购买加量

常见问题

AI 识别或诊断失败

  • 请使用光线充足、植物主体清晰的照片。
  • 检查网络连接是否正常。
  • 服务端繁忙或限流时请稍后重试。
  • 免费或当月 AI 次数可能已用完，请在应用内查看订阅/加量说明。

收不到养护提醒

  • 在系统设置中允许「花小绿」发送通知。
  • 在植物详情「养护手册」中为对应项目开启提醒。

诊断时的位置权限

位置权限可选，仅在您开始 AI 诊断并同意授权时使用， 用于结合当地季节与近期天气。拒绝授权仍可正常诊断，只是不含气候上下文。

订阅与恢复购买

  • 订阅与内购由 Apple 扣款。
  • 重装或换机后，请在应用内付费页使用恢复购买。
  • 退款需联系 Apple 客服，开发者无法直接退款。

如何删除数据

花园数据保存在本机。在应用内删除植物，或卸载应用即可清除本地数据。 我们不运营存储您花园内容的用户账号服务器。
